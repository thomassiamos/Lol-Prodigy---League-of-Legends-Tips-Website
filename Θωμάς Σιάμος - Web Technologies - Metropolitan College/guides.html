Gameplay Guides

Master the fundamentals to climb faster.

Wave Management

  • Freeze: Keep 3-4 enemy minions for CS lead.
  • Slow Push: Crash at 3:00 for roam timing.
  • Fast Push: Clear by 1:30 for reset.

Objective Control

  • Drake: Ward at 4:30, take at 5:00.
  • Baron: Vision at 24:00, secure at 25:00.
  • Herald: Pre-14:00 for 400g.

Teamfight Basics

  • ADC: Position at 700 range.
  • Tank: Engage with CC (e.g., Leona).
  • Mage: Burst priority targets (e.g., Ekko).
