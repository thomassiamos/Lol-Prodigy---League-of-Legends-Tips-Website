Lane Roles

Understand each role and its meta champions.

Top Lane

Fighter/Tank - Win lane, split push.

Meta: Darius (52.5% WR)

Jungle

Ganker/Tank - Secure objectives.

Meta: Xin Zhao (53.0% WR)

Mid Lane

Mage/Assassin - Burst, roam.

Meta: Ekko (52.1% WR)

Bot Lane

Marksman - Farm, deal DPS.

Meta: Jhin (51.8% WR)

Support
